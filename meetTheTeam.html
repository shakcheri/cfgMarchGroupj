Shakila Cherise Joyner

Shakila Cherise Joyner is a psychologist, lecturer, and researcher.

Her work in the field of Human-Computer Interaction is situated at the intersection of technology and the dimensions of diversity that shape the human experience. Constantly aimed at causing practical impact her work highlights the appreciation of interdependencies, demands and attributes accountability, and strives to promote equity through technology. Her latest research revolves around accessible data visualizations for people with visual impairments.

Skills: UX/UI research, Softwaredevelopement (Java), Web development, Database development (SQL), Statistical computing (SPSS, R) , Datavisualization, HCI research
